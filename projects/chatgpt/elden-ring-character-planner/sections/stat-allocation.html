Stat Allocation

Level Requirement: Lv. 1

Stat Points Allocated
Vigor 10
Mind 10
Endurance 10
Strength 10
Dexterity 10
Intelligence 10
Faith 10
Arcane 10

Vigor: Increases maximum HP. Softcaps at 50, hardcaps at 99.

Mind: Increases maximum FP. Softcaps at 40, hardcaps at 99.

Endurance: Increases maximum stamina and equip load. Softcaps at 40, hardcaps at 99.

Strength: Increases physical attack power for strength-based weapons. Softcaps at 40, hardcaps at 99.

Dexterity: Increases physical attack power for dexterity-based weapons. Softcaps at 40, hardcaps at 99.

Intelligence: Increases magic attack power for intelligence-based weapons and spell damage. Softcaps at 40, hardcaps at 99.

Faith: Increases lightning attack power for faith-based weapons and spell damage. Softcaps at 40,hardcaps at 99.

Arcane: Increases fire and arcane attack power for arcane-based weapons and spell damage. Softcaps at 40, hardcaps at 99.
